Privacy Policy — PostReady IG

Last updated: 17 July 2026 · Package: com.dcpstudio.postready

PostReady IG (“we”, “the app”) helps users prepare photos and videos for social-media publishing. The app is not affiliated with Meta or Instagram.

Data we process

  • Photos and videos selected by users are processed on-device for resizing, cropping, adjustment, watermarking, splitting, and compression. We do not upload this media to our servers.
  • The camera is accessed only when the user chooses to capture a photo or video.
  • Preferences, Pro entitlement state, free-tier quotas, and optional export history are stored locally on the device.
  • Google ML Kit face/object detection may run on-device for smart cropping; the app does not send selected media to our servers.

Network use and third parties

  • Google Play Billing processes purchases and subscription status. Google's privacy policy applies to payment information.
  • An optional action may download a public image-specification configuration over HTTPS.
  • The current build contains no advertising, analytics, or crash-reporting SDK.

Permissions

Internet access supports Play Billing and optional specification updates. Camera access is used only for user-initiated capture. Media access primarily uses Android's system Photo Picker and share interfaces.

Data sharing, retention, and deletion

We do not sell user data. The app has no user accounts and does not collect user data on our servers. Local app data can be deleted by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app. Exported files can be deleted by the user through their device.

Subscriptions

PostReady IG Pro is managed through Google Play. Billing, cancellation, and refund handling are provided by Google Play.

Children

The app is intended for adults and is not directed to children.

Contact

Privacy questions: Chandradcp26@gmail.com.

Changes

We may update this policy when app features or legal requirements change. The latest revision date will appear on this page.
